Parking

Safety

Smoking is not permitted indoors; however, it is permitted solely on the balcony. Smoke detectors are installed in every room.

In the event of a fire, a fire extinguisher can be found in the upper section of the black display cabinet near the entrance to the living room.

Additionally, a first aid kit is also stored in the same cabinet.

Bathroom

Please ensure that toilet paper is disposed of exclusively in the toilet. For all other waste, there is a trash can available in the bathroom and another one located next to the toilet.
